About Our Server

Our Hearts of Minecraft server brings the strategic depth of Hearts of Iron IV into the blocky world of Minecraft. With custom mods including Flan's WW2 pack, faction systems, and realistic combat mechanics, we offer a unique multiplayer experience unlike anything else.

What We Offer

Join our community and experience:

  • Custom faction mod with persistent territories
  • Modified recipes and crafting systems
  • Realistic vehicle fuel efficiency and mechanics
  • Custom weapon models and animations
  • Active staff and regular events

Community

We have a vibrant community of WW2 and Minecraft enthusiasts. Join our Discord to get the modpack, connect with other players, report bugs, and stay updated on new features and events.
